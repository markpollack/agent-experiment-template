## Summary

Implemented **slice 3 of 5** of the cross-repo "first-class journal capture" feature: made the
**canonical agent-journal capture on-by-default** for every experiment bootstrapped from this template.
The behaviour came for free — experiment-core (slice 2) makes journaling a property of *running an
experiment* (default-on when an `outputDir` is configured), and the template already wired
`.outputDir(results)`. So the work was: consume the SNAPSHOT deps, make the default **visible**,
**guard** it with a committed regression test, and **repoint the analysis-layer guidance** off the
retired result-store ETL (`load_results.py`) onto the canonical journal (`load_journal`), carrying the
allocation-not-measurement cost caveat. Author writes zero journal code. `./mvnw test` green.

Canonical contract (READ-ONLY here): `~/projects/agent-journal/plans/journal-capture-DESIGN.md`.
Handoff: `plans/inbox/journal-capture-handoff.md`.
